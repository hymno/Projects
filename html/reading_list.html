The Classics

These two were both published in 1935 and at the time had apparently nothing to do with each other, but are now the two paper's involved in the conjecture proposed by Maldacena, Susskind (2013) that wormhole formation can come about by the entanglement of two black holes; thus, ER=EPR.

  • Einstein, Podolsky, Rosen. Published in PRL March 25, 1935: Can Quantum-Mechanical Description of Physical Reality Be Considered Complete?
  • Einstein, Rosen. Published in PRL May 8 1935: The Particle Problem in the General Theory of Relativity
  • Hawking, 1975: Particle Creation by Black Holes
Modern Wonders
  • Juan Maldacena, 1997: The Large N Limit of Superconformal field theories and supergravity
In The Trenches
